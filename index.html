About The Tour

Days 1 - 3

Delhi & Taj Mahal Heritage

Arrive in New Delhi, the historic capital. Excursion to Agra to witness the striking sunrise beauty of the Taj Mahal and visit the massive Agra Fort.

Days 4 - 6

Jaipur Pink City Palaces

Board the high-speed Vande Bharat train to Jaipur. Wander through the mesmerizing Hawa Mahal, Amer Fort, and experience authentic Rajasthani heritage.

Days 7 - 10

Kerala Backwaters & Houseboats

End your journey in the tranquil greenery of Kerala. Experience a traditional tea plantation walk and relax on an overnight houseboat cruise in Alleppey.
