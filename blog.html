Seasonal Packing Guides

Pack waterproof jackets, quick-dry clothing, sandals, insect repellents, and sealed dry bags for phones.

Dress conservatively when visiting active temples/mosques. Cover shoulders and knees, and pack slip-on shoes.

Travel Advisory & Tips

Always verify schedule timing of heritage monuments before leaving. Taj Mahal remains closed to tourists on Fridays.
