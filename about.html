Redefining Curated Travel

Founded in Bandra Kurla Complex (BKC) Mumbai, Stackly arose from a simple mission: to rescue discerning travelers from generic, crowded tour packages. We design unique, private excursions guided by expert local companions who love sharing their regional history.
